Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

A mini digital edition
Made by Xinran Li
As a project for the course Text as Data, taught at the University of Antwerp

Welcome to a digital edition of chapter 7A of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley's Frankenstein from the Bodleian MS. Abinger c.56 manuscript. Written in late 1816, these pages are part of the surviving draft of this famous novel, which was published in 1818. While Mary Shelley's handwriting dominates the manuscript, edits by Percy Shelley are also visible throughout, providing an insight into their collaboration and the development of the text.

The 10 pages in this edition are presented in the order they appear in the manuscript:

  • Folio 21r (p.75)
  • Folio 21v (p.76)
  • Folio 22r (p.77)
  • Folio 22v (p.78)
  • Folio 23r (p.79)
  • Folio 23v (p.80)
  • Folio 24r (p.81)
  • Folio 24v (p.82)
  • Folio 25r (p.83)
  • Folio 25v (p.84)
